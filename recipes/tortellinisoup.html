Reddit Tortellini Man aka Meme Soup

Description

This is an extremely popular soup on reddit, intended for a slow cooker where you just jam all the shit in and roll. This is meant for a dutch oven, but any big pot is fine. It's a red soup that has some veggies thrown in with sausage and tortellini, with cream and flour added for thickening.

Ingredients

  • 1 yellow onion, diced
  • 0.25 cup of flour
  • EVOO
  • salt and pepper
  • red pepper flakes
  • A lot of garlic. A LOT.
  • Italian spices, rosemary, thyme, basil, oregano
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbls tomato paste
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 28 oz roasted canned tomatoes
  • MSG
  • parmesan rind
  • zuchini, broccoli, mushrooms
  • bag of tortellini
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • hot italian sausage or dong of your liking

Cooking Steps

  1. Brown the sausage in olive oil, then set aside.
  2. Fry onion in olive oil for as long as you'd like. Add garlic for 2-3 minutes when the onions are near done.
  3. Make a little clear space to fry the spices in olive oil, about 30 seconds.
  4. Fry the flour in the oil for a few minutes, stirring it.
  5. Fry the tomato paste for 1-2 mins, then add tomatos, then add broth.
  6. Add the sausage, parmesan rind, and bay leaves back in once it's burbling.
  7. Cook this for at least 0.5 hours, Ashlee does less Travis do longer.
  8. 10 mins before completion, add heavy cream, zuchini, broccoli, and mushrooms.
  9. When you are satisfied with the outcome (TASTE IT!!!!!!!!!! ADD SALT (and maybe drops of red wine vinegar)) add tortellini for the suggested time.
  • Really you can freestyle on any of the cook times or veggies, the OG recipe suggests spinach. I don't. Add a chile of your choice. or ten.
