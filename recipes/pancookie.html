10 Minute Pan Cookie

Description

An ideal recipe for when you're impaired and desperate for dessert. It can be combined in about 10 mins and bakes for 15 mins. Mix in your own toppings and love life. You will need a small iron skillet which might be annoying but just go thrifting and win.

Ingredients

  • 0.25 cup melted butter
  • 2 tbls granulated sugar
  • 3 tbls brown sugar
  • 1 egg yolk ( SO annoying Ashlee usually (always) does this part )
  • 1 tea of vanila
  • 0.25 tea of baking soda
  • a pinch of salt
  • 0.5 cup flour
  • 0.33 cup chocy chip
  • flaky salt for the top
  • (optional(??!?!?!??)) nuts of your favoring

Cooking steps

  1. Preheat to 350F
  2. Melt the butter in the skillet, which also lubes it up.
  3. In a small mixing bowl, combine melted butter, both sugars, the yolk, vanilla, baking soda, and salt
  4. Stir in the flour, then the chocolate chips.
  5. Top with flaky salt and bake for 13-15 minutes.
