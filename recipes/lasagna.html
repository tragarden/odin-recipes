Lasagna Bolognese

Description

Lasanga Bolognese is an alternative to the red sauce lasagna we all recognize. Utilizing a classic ragu, this meat heavy dish is known for being very filling. Ricotta and fresh meat aplenty, PLEASE try it out with freshly home-ground meat if possible!

Ingredients

Ragu Bolognese

  • 2 Tablespoons of EVOO
  • 4 Tablespoons butter
  • 1lb ground pork
  • 4oz chicken livers
  • 1 onion
  • 2 large carrots
  • 3 celery ribs
  • 1 head of garlic
  • handful of sage leaves
  • hella red pepper flakes (don't be scared)
  • 1 can of San Marzano tomatoes
  • 1 cup of red wine
  • 1 cup of Whole milk
  • 2 cups of homemade chicken stock
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tablespoon of fish sauce
  • 0.5 cups of heavy cream
  • salt and pepper(s here)
  • handful of basil leaves

Cooking steps

Ragu Bolognese

  1. Fry the meat in butter and olive oil.
  2. When the meat is no longer pink, remove it and add the veggies.
  3. Return the meat to the cookpot, add wine, milk, tomatoes, stock, and bay leaves.
  4. DO NOT SKIM THE FAT OFF THE TOP WHY WOULD YOU DO THAT OMG
  5. While this is simmering, begin work on the ricotta chese part of the recipe.
